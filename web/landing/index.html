Consent-first voice review for supervised education.

V.O.T Guardian is a SecuredMe Education surface for teaching signal quality, consent, uncertainty, evidence gaps, and human review. It extracts acoustic variables needed for AI-versus-human review without needing to know what was said, then Tenebris purges private audio and discussion content.

Architecture boundary

Tenebris is the erasure protocol after V.O.T captures the variables it needs.

Tenebris purge protocol

Present Tenebris as the post-capture erasure layer: V.O.T keeps the acoustic variables needed for review, then purges private audio and discussion content instead of retaining what was said.

  • VOT, jitter, shimmer, SNR, THD, ZCR, centroid, MFCC mean.
  • Private content erased after variable capture.
  • Target purge window: within 129 ms.

Planned Cactus capsule

Cactus belongs to the planned on-device or ephemeral inference direction. It is shown as future architecture, not as already implanted in V.O.T Guardian. Its role is to help keep the sensitive context short-lived around the same Tenebris purge doctrine.

  • Short-lived review context.
  • Tenebris purge boundary.
  • No device-wide surveillance claim.

Defensive education, fraud-awareness learning, consent-first review, variable extraction, Tenebris erasure, and supervised evidence discussion.

What it is not

Not biometric identification, not policing, not compliance proof, not discipline, not emergency response.
